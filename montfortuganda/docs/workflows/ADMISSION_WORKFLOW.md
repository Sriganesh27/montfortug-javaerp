# ADMISSION WORKFLOW

*Status: Verified Enterprise Workflow*

This document serves as the exact enterprise reference for the Montfort Uganda ERP Admission Workflow, detailing the 14-step progression from public application to final enrollment.

## STEP 1
Parent submits admission application. (Currently completed.)
↓
## STEP 2
Application appears in Admission Officer Dashboard.
Admission Officer can:
- View Application
- Verify Documents
- Verify Eligibility
- Return for Correction
- Reject
- Assign Teacher/HOD
↓
## STEP 3
Assigned Teacher/HOD receives application.
Teacher can:
- View Application
- Conduct Interview
- Conduct Entrance Test
- Enter Test Marks
- Enter Interview Remarks
- Enter Recommendation

*Note: Teacher CANNOT approve students. Teacher only recommends.*
↓
## STEP 4
Teacher submits recommendation.
Application automatically returns to Admission Officer.
↓
## STEP 5
Admission Officer reviews:
- Application
- Teacher Recommendation
- Test Marks
- Interview Report

Admission Officer decides:
- Selected
- Waitlisted
- Rejected
↓
## STEP 6
System sends Email to Parent.
(If Selected)
↓
Parents visit school.
↓
## STEP 7
Admission Officer verifies original documents physically.
↓
## STEP 8
Fee Discussion and rules discussion.
Decision: Can parent pay?
- **YES** ↓ Proceed to Payment.
- **NO** ↓ Admission Officer requests Scholarship.
  
*System should support two options:*
- Option 1: Admission Officer creates Scholarship Request.
- Option 2: System sends Scholarship Application Link to parent's primary email. (Recommended Enterprise approach)
↓
## STEP 9
Parent submits Scholarship Application.
Uploads:
- Income Proof
- Recommendation Letter
- Medical Documents
- Supporting Documents
↓
## STEP 10
School Admin reviews Scholarship. (Most verification happens here.)
School Admin can:
- Verify Documents
- Verify Income
- Verify Eligibility
- Request More Information
- Reject
- Recommend
↓
## STEP 11
Only verified applications reach Super Admin.
Super Admin reviews:
- School Recommendation
- Financial Verification
- Supporting Documents

Scholarship Decision:
- 100%
- 75%
- 50%
- 25%
- Rejected
↓
## STEP 12
Parent receives Scholarship Decision Email.
↓
## STEP 13
Fee Payment.
- If Scholarship Approved:
  - ↓ completely no fees payment
  - else if some amount is allocated ↓ Remaining Fee Payment.
- Else:
  - ↓ Full Fee Payment.
↓
## STEP 14
Enrollment.
Automatically create:
- Student
- Parent Account
- Student Login
- Student Profile
- Roll Number
- Admission Number
- Class Assignment
- Section Assignment
- Fee Account

Application becomes Student. Admission process completed.
